About Us

ISDCTT or Internet Software Development Company, Trade and Technologies. It is a multinational technology and trading company specialized in internet-based services and products. We are currently working extensively on information technology. Among our IT services - Web development, Android software, Content solutions, Digital marketing, SEO services, Social media ads, Multimedia solutions, and Digital publications. We manage a number of websites and business organizations, including e-commerce sites.

Our Mission: Our mission is to work for the human world with the proper use of information technology and to ensure the right quality of our services and products.

Our Vision: ISDCTT will always work on creative and advanced technological innovation. Each of its business operations will be managed with integrity and maintaining high standards and it will work to achieve international prosperity.
